History of Burhanpur Railway Station

Burhanpur Madhya Pradesh ka ek prachin aur aitihasik sheher hai. Mughal kaal me ye sheher trade aur military movement ka important center tha. Burhanpur Railway Station ne British era ke dauran is sheher ko desh ke anya hisson se jodne me bahut badi bhumika nibhayi. Tapti nadi ke kinare sthit ye station aaj bhi North-South railway route ka important junction mana jata hai.

Yeh station sirf yatra ka madhyam nahi, balki sheher ke vikas, rozgar aur tourism ka pramukh kendra raha hai. Aaj Burhanpur Railway Station modern facilities ke saath heritage aur technology ka perfect combination hai.
